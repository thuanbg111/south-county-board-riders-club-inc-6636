About South County Board Riders, Club, Inc.

South County Board Riders, Club, Inc. is a charitable organization recognized as a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 001756636). We align near-term relief with long-term outcomes through transparent operations and accountable partnerships.

Relief

Immediate support that reduces harm and connects people to services.

Income

Housing

Activation of land to create safe interim sites and paths to permanence.
